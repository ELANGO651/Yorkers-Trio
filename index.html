Ad sales insights provider
Proof Of Concept

Helping advertisers to track and optimize their ad campaigns. The platform might offer a wide range of features and functionality, such as the ability to track ad performance across multiple channels, customizable reporting, and integration with ad platforms. Advertisers can use the platform to access data on metrics such as clicks, impressions, conversion rate, and cost per acquisition, and to make informed decisions about their ad campaigns based on this data. An ad insights platform can help advertisers to save money on their ad spend by providing tools and insights for optimizing their campaigns. Overall, the goal of an ad insights platform is to provide advertisers with the data and tools they need to effectively and efficiently reach their target audience and achieve their advertising goals.
